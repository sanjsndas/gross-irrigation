2. Description of Services

Gross Irrigation provides a range of smart, water-efficient irrigation solutions for residential, commercial, and agricultural clients. Our services include:

2.1. Drip & Sprinkler Installations

We offer professional design and installation of customized drip and sprinkler irrigation systems. This includes initial site assessment, soil-type-based planning, system layout, component selection (emitters, sprinklers, valves, pipes), and complete installation. Our installations are compatible with various environments, including lawns, terraces, vertical walls, and balcony gardens. All installations are performed by certified technicians using high-quality, durable materials. We integrate advanced app-controlled timers and zone systems to ensure precise and efficient water delivery.

2.2. Water Use Optimization

Our optimization services focus on converting outdated or inefficient irrigation systems to modern, water-saving technologies. This involves auditing existing systems, identifying leaks, and upgrading components to enhance water efficiency. We install smart meters for real-time water usage insights and implement leak detection and auto shutoff technologies to prevent waste and damage. The goal is to maximize efficiency, reduce water consumption, and lower utility costs for our clients.

2.3. Rainwater System Integration

We design and implement solutions to integrate your irrigation system with rainwater harvesting setups. This includes connecting to rooftop tanks, installing filtration systems, and ensuring proper storage and distribution of collected rainwater. We also offer greywater reuse compatibility, enabling the repurposing of household greywater for irrigation purposes. Our systems are designed with overflow and backup safety mechanisms to ensure continuous operation and prevent property damage.

2.4. Zone-Based Irrigation Planning

We provide expert planning for multi-zone irrigation systems, tailoring watering schedules and flow rates based on specific garden areas. This includes mapping sun/shade exposure, identifying plant-specific water requirements, and custom programming by season. Our aim is to ensure optimal hydration for diverse plant types within your landscape, minimizing water waste and promoting healthier plant growth.

2.5. Maintenance Plans

To ensure the longevity and peak performance of your irrigation system, we offer comprehensive maintenance plans. These plans typically include quarterly visits for system inspection, adjustment, troubleshooting, and preventative care. We also provide emergency support within a specified timeframe (e.g., 24 hours) and offer no-hassle parts replacement for components covered under the plan.

2.6. Digital Tools and Resources

Our services extend to providing access to digital tools such as the Irrigation Cost Calculator, Mobile App Dashboard for live water data and reports, Rain Delay Mode, and Smart Timer Wizard. We also offer an Educational Hub with articles and micro-tutorials to empower users with knowledge about smart irrigation practices.
